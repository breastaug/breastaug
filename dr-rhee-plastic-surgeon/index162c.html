Are there any natural solutions to get rid of saggy breasts at a young age? (Photos)
Apr 6, 2020
Unfortunately, as you have heard from others, there likely is not a great non-surgical method to reshape your breasts.  The good news, however, is that you will, most likely be able to address the sagging, areolar size, and symmetry with surgery.  You will need an in person exam with a board certified physician, who can determine the appropriate procedure for you.  You will have the 'anchor' type scars, which are noticeable early, but after they fade, will be quite acceptable.Good luck!

Care Credit - Breast Implant Financing Options
Financing and Care Credit
May 5, 2019
Financing options will vary by practice.In general, it is advisable to have a reasonable health insurance policy in place before any elective surgery.  Any surgery carries some degree of risk with it, and on the off chance that you require medical care following your procedure, it’s helpful to have health insurance that will cover complications if they occur.
What’s the best silicone implant that offers the most fullness and projection with no sagging?
Best silicone implant
May 5, 2019
There really is no ‘best’ silicone implant.  This is why there are so many different types of implants available.  Each surgeon will have their own personal preference.However, I would advise you that your end outcome is much more a reflection of other factors vs the actual implant type. In other words, your physical body habitus prior to surgery and the size of the implant will likely contribute more to your end outcome than the type of implant.

Itchy burning skin 5 weeks post breast implants. What could this be?
Itching after breast augmentation
May 5, 2019
As others have outlined, it is most likely that the itching that you are experiencing is a result of nerve stretch injury during your procedure.  If so, this should be a temporary condition that ultimately resolves. However, it is best to be evaluated by your surgeon, as there are other conditions that potentially would contribute to this.
Would breast implants work/sit right on my deformed ribcage with pectus excavatum?
Breast Asymmetry
May 5, 2019
It would be best to have an idea of what your ideal outcome would be.It’s unlikely that implants alone, of any type are going to provide you with reasonable symmetry.  More than likely, you are going to require some version of a mastopexy in addition to implants for an attempt at symmetry.  Gel implants are generally going to be more forgiving of chest wall asymmetry such as yours.Best of luck.

Can a floated down belly button from a mini tummy tuck be lifted to a more normal location? (Pho
Can a floated down belly button from a mini tummy tuck be lifted to a more normal location? (Photo)
Aug 22, 2018
This is one of the issues with a floating umbilicus tummy tuck.  Pulling the skin tight can result in a low belly button.  Keeping the belly button in a 'natural' position usually involves a compromise in how tight the skin is post procedure.  The ideal candidate for a floating umbilicus tummy tuck has fascial laxity as the main issue with little/no skin excess.
