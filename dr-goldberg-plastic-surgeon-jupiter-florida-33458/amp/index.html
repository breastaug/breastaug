I have a dent on my upper cheek under my eye. Would a filler be able to fix this?
Dent under the eye when smiling
Jul 18, 2020
This area is called the “lid cheek junction” and it is a tricky thing to treat sometimes.

The main cause is losing volume (fat and collagen) in the cheeks from aging, sun damage, weight loss, or movement of the cheeks over time.

You may benefit from filler, collagen stimulating treatments like ultherapy or radio frequency microneedling, or laser treatments. I recommend an in person consultation with an experienced injector/laser aesthetician.

Will Botox help my nose from pointing down when I smile?
Botox to the nasal depressor muscle
Jul 12, 2020
You will likely get some subtle improvement by using a few units of Botox between your nose and upper lip to weaken the muscle that pulls down the top of the nose. If this doesn’t work, I would skip filler and just consider rhinoplasty. It’s the most extreme of your options, but it’s also permanent. Good luck

What happens if your breast implant diameter is smaller than your BWD?
Base width and breast implants
Jan 24, 2020
Base width is important to me when I pick implants for patients, but 0.5cm is not a large difference and wouldn’t even be noticeable to most people. It’s only when the base width is off by 2-3cm that some patients develop issues with the implant filling out the entire cleavage. It depends on where the implant is placed and how much breast tissue someone has.
