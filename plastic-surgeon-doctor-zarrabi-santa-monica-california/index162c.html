What is Breast Augmentation Mastopexy?
Breast Augmentation Mastopexy
Apr 12, 2018
A mastopexy is a breast lift. If the position of the nipple and areola have become too low, then they will need to be lifted up. Most patients who have not had children or have not had major weight loss will not require a lift. An in person consultation is the best way to know if you would need a lift and if so, what type of lift you would benefit from.
