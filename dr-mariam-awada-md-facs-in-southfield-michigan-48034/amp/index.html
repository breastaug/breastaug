Is it normal for my implants to still look like this 6 weeks out? What can I do?
Is it normal for my implants to still look like this 6 weeks out? What can I do? (Photos)
Dec 4, 2019
It can take a while for implants to "drop" into their pockets in the breast after surgery. Sometimes a breast band is used after surgery, because the implants will want to ride up on the chest wall until the soft tissue has a chance to relax. Be patient and follow the advice of your surgeon. You may want to ask if there is anything else that can be done to speed up the process.  Good luck to you!
