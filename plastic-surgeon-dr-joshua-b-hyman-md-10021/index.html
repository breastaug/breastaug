How long will arm pain last after breast implants?
Sore Arms after Breast Augmentation is Common
Nov 13, 2018
Sore arms after breast augmentation is very common. When the implants are placed under the muscle it causes muscle stretch and muscle spasm. The chest muscle is also attached to the arm laterally. I place all my breast augmentations on muscle relaxants after surgery. In addition, I perform a long acting anesthetic block on the muscle prior to leaving the operating room. There are definitely ways to make this procedure more comfortable.
