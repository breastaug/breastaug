What does it mean when Botox weakens muscles?
Botox pathophysiology
Jun 30, 2020
Hi,Thank you for your questions.Botox and Dysport work by disrupting the communication between nerve cells and muscle cells. This is an irreversible process at the neuromuscular junction but eventually new nerve terminals will develop over the next few months.By preventing/reducing muscular movement, this reduces the pull on the overlying skin resulting in decreased wrinkles. Hope this helps!
