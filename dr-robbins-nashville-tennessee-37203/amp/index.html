Will I be able to take care of myself after a tummy tuck?
taking care of yourself after tummy tuck
Jul 23, 2020
Hello and thank you for your question. It depends on your pain tolerance and how much medication you will need. If you're medicated with pain meds , you won't know when you took what medication, if there are stairs , you need to be careful not to fall if medicated, and in the shower. I would have an alarm set up for your med schedule, have meals made , a lot of fluids, drinks , electrolytes on hand as well. Your phone nearby in case you need to call someone.
