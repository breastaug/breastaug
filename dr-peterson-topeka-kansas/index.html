Could masseter Botox for TMJ have an effect on the eustachian tube?
TMJ and eustachian tube dysfunction
Mar 3, 2020
The muscles that control your eustachian tubes are locate in your soft palate, primarily the tensor veli palatini and should not be effected by injection of the masseter muscle, which is the muscle usually injected to treat TMJ. I have injected several patients for TMJ and have never had anyone tell me that it adversely effected the eustachian tube function. I hope this helps. Best of luck.
