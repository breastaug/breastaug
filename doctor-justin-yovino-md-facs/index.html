Do I need axillary lipo along with my implants?
Armpit Front Bra Roll Lipo
Jun 17, 2019
Liposuction is typically the best approach for this area.  Breast implants will improve the breast but does not directly change the axillary (armpit) or front bra roll region.  Occasionally, after a breast augmentation, there is a rebalancing of these areas and it becomes less of a concern visually.  Thorough smooth complete liposuction of axilla and front bra rolls requires a specialized technique that includes specialized powered microcannulas, multiple positions, and significant experience.
