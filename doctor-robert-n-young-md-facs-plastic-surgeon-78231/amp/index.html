How does a micro-autologous fat transfer support the lower eyelids and help to prevent hollow
Micro-autologous fat transfer
Nov 27, 2019
Hello and thank you for your question.  Micro-autologous fat transfer has become an alternative treatment for lower eyelid blepharoplasty.  The traditional blepharoplasty removes fatty tissue and this sometimes still results in a hollowness between the eyelid and cheek junction.  The fat transfer tends to blend the eyelid with the cheek junction making traditional excision unnecessary.  This should not disrupt the position of your lower lid.  Best of luck to you.
