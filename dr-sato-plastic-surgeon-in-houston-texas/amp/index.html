Breastfeeding. Is there any way to keep your milk supply while getting a BBL?
Breastfeeding. Is there any way to keep your milk supply while getting a BBL?
Jul 12, 2020
You should wait until you are at least 6 weeks out from breast feeding and at least 6 month post partum. The extra estrogen that circulates in your blood during pregnancy and breast feeding can thicken your blood and put you at increased risk for a blood clot (DVT/PE). 
Is it safe to take steroids before Brazilian butt lift?
Is it safe to take steroids before Brazilian butt lift?
Jul 12, 2020
Corticosteroids affect your immune system and your ability to fight infection. I will not perform a BBL on a patient who is actively taking steroids. If you are put on a dose pack or short term treatment, make sure you share this with your PS. You need to have them all of the way out of your system before surgery. 
I have terrible hip dips! Can this be fixed with a BBL? I’m 5’8” and 191 lbs
I have terrible hip dips! Can this be fixed with a BBL? I’m 5’8” and 191 lbs
Jul 12, 2020
Yes, a BBL corrects hip dips. In my practice, this is the most common reason women seek this procedure out. After a BBL, ~50% of the fat volume is lost over 3-6 months, so there is a chance that the hip dips will be fully corrected immediately post op but may come back slightly (not as strong as they are now) as some of the fat volume is lost.
