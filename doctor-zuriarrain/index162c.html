Can a breast lift, augmentation and BBL be done at once?
BBL/BA
Apr 17, 2019
I recommend that the procedure be performed separately. The recovery does not make any sense if you have both surgeries done at the same time. You will not be able to lay on your back/buttock after a BBL and you will not be able to lay on your chest/abdomen. Therefore, if you want the best results you should do these procedures separately.
