What is the likelihood of a Breast Augmentation complication?
What is the likelihood of a Breast Augmentation complication?
Nov 26, 2018
As with any surgery, there is always a risk of complications. Capsular contracture, double bubble deformity, and bottoming out can happen but generally speaking, breast augmentation has a high rate of patient satisfaction. While most patients eventually do require a revision (due to need for implant change, lift, etc.), these are not complications but rather limitations of the implant and the fact that gravity always wins! Good luck
