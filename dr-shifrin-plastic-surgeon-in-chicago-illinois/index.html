Feb 27, 2020
The answer is it depends on your heart health. If your cardiomyopathy is severe and your ejection fraction is impaired I would recommend against having any elective surgery. On the other hand, if it’s minor and your cardiologist believes you are safe for the surgery you want and you have a full workup before surgery prooving your heart is healthy you can have it. It’s needs to be approached with caution in any curcumstance.Best
