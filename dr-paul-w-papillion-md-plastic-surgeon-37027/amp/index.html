Breast
Dec 3, 2018
Hello, yes...but it would look horrible. Part of breast augmentation is matching the breast tissue with the implant. If you just put an implant in when you need a lift... you have a full looking upper breast, with the remaining breast tissue hanging off of the implant. Does not look good. Seek out a good surgeon for eval.Dr P
