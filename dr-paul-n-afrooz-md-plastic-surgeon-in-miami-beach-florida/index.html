Do bones fuse back together after nasal osteotomy?
Nasal Bone Osteotomies
May 30, 2019
Ostoeomies are essentially very precise and controlled cuts of the bone which free the bones such that they can be repositioned into a more cosmetically-pleasing position.  These osteotomies do heal just like any other bone in the body and achieve almost normal strength by 6-8 weeks following surgery.  Often times after rhinoplasty, patients are advised not to have anything rest on the nose such as glasses.  Part of the reason for this is because the bones are still healing for up to 8 weeks.
