Can liposuction and tummy tuck be done at the same time?
Yes
Jul 14, 2020
Yes, liposuction is often done at the same time as a tummy tuck. The tummy tuck tightens the abdominal muscle while removing fat and skin from the stomach, however it does not correct any fullness in the lovehandles or bra strap region of the posterior trunk. Many doctors recommend doing the 2 together so that the patient does not have to go through multiple procedures to achieve the results they are looking for.

How to get old figure back after having kids. Will I need lipo or a tummy tuck?
Tummy Tuck
Jun 16, 2020
I would recommend doing a tummy tuck because that procedure includes correcting the diastasis, which is the muscle that separates after child bearing. In addition to the muscle tightening, a tummy tuck also includes liposuction and and skin excision. Liposuction alone does not correct the muscle separation. As for the breast lift, that is completely doable without implants. Please call us at 6019360905 if you have any further questions!

Overweight and Breast Reduction Surgery: Worth Doing?
It is not abnormal to get a breast reduction when you are overweight.
Jun 9, 2020
It is not abnormal to get a breast reduction when you are overweight. Many doctors recommend losing weight before the procedure, however reducing the size of your breasts may jumpstart a healthier lifestyle.
