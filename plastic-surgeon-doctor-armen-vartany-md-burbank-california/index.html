There may be a number of causes possible causes for your breathing problem.  You may have untreated septal deviation, internal or external valve collapse, untreated inferior turbinate enlargement or an allergic condition. I want to remove this 4 day old tattoo, can it be removed completely without any scars?
Laser Tattoo Removal
Oct 11, 2018
The tattoo can be removed with serial laser treatments, however, you need to wait about 6 weeks from the date you got the tattoo before you can start the treatments.  Try to find a place that uses the newer "pico" type of laser which is more effective than the older "nano" lasers. Scarring is very rare after laser tattoo removal, but a slight ghost of the tattoo usually remains due to pigment alterations in the treated skin.
