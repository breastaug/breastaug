Red Bump at Middle of Tummy Tuck Incision Normal ?
suture abscess after tummy tuck
Mar 28, 2013
I would recommend calling your PS and finding out who is covering the practice. It is possible to have a suture abscess where an almost pimple forms around one of the sutures. It is easilty resolved with removal of that suture piece and antibiotic cream. It is important to have it looked at to avoid the incision opening up a bit. I hope this helps you.

Kindest regards,

Neil J. Zemmel

One Implant Riding Very High After Breast Augmentation With Lift?

In regards to the pain, most of my patients have one breast that is more sore than the other, usually their dominant hand side. The pain will get better! I hope this helps you.

Tanning and Breast Reduction?
tanning after breast reduction
Feb 18, 2013
Tanning can be harmful to scars for a full year after surgery. If you are going to get in the sun or lay in the tanning bed make sure your breasts are covered with a high spf sunscreen, zinc oxide. If you do not, the scars may come in raised and hyperpigmented. I hope this helps you.

Kindest regards,

Neil j. Zemmel
