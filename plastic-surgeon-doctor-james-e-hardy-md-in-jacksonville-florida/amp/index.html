What happens if I do CoolSculpting and then treat over part of the area with Kybella?
Nov 18, 2019
Hi and thanks for the question. You will get a result still with Kybella in the CoolSculpting area. Both treatments result in the elimination of fat cells by different mechanisms. The biggest issue resulting from doing both within a short period of time is that some of the fat cells that will be removed as a result of CoolSculpting may still be present when you applied the Kybella. If you had waited you would have had a bigger effect on the remaining cells. Best of Luck - Dr. Hardy...

What's the best fajas garments for BBL?
What's the best fajas garments for BBL?
Feb 2, 2019
Hi and thanks for the question. BBL garments are specific to your surgeon. I insist on garments that do not compress the buttocks and hips with either the buttocks and hips completely cut out or a very stretchy material over them. The buttocks and hips need to stretch over a period of at least 3-4 months. if a garment is tight over this area it can limit how much the tissue in these areas can stretch and therefore limit the size and shape. Best of Luck - Dr. Hardy...
