Can I do lipo on my abdoman after my tummy tuck from 2 yrs ago?
Liposuction after tummy tuck
Nov 25, 2019
Most conservative and well trained plastic surgeons do not do aggressive liposuction of the abdomen at the same time as tummy tuck for fear of creating major healing problems with the abdominal flap. Once the flap has had time to heal and revascularize, it is perfectly safe to then do liposuction.I generally wait at least 6 months after tummy tuck to make sure there has been enough time for healing.
