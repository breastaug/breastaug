Why do I have a perky firm left breast and to a right normal soft breast 9 months post-op?
Likely Cause of Hardened Breast
Nov 6, 2018
From your description and photo, it sounds like you have probably developed capsular contracture in your left breast. This can happen to anyone, and is not a result of shocks from your phone. I recommend visiting your plastic surgeon as soon as possible to discuss your options. Capsular contracture can be treated with medications, ultrasound, or surgery, depending on how advanced it has become.
