How Long After Breast Reduction Surgery Are Breasts Swollen?
Breast reduction swelling
Feb 5, 2020
It takes at least several months for breast reduction swelling to completely resolve.  Follow your plastic surgeon’s advice and give it time.  If you feel your breasts are still too large after the swelling has resolved, a small procedure much easier than the first surgery can usually help make them smaller. 
How Much Does Breast Reduction Cost?
Breast reduction cost
Feb 5, 2020
The cost of a breast reduction will vary depending on your location and the extent of your surgery.  There are accurate ranges listed on realself as well as the ASAPS and ASPS websites, I suggest you look at those, then consult with board certified PS’s in your area.

Pubic Area Appears Bigger After Tummy Tuck
Mons pubis after tummy tuck
Feb 5, 2020
It is very common for the mons pubis to be swollen following a tummy tuck and it can take months for it to completely resolve.  I would allow the swelling to completely resolve before making a decision about the size of your mons pubis.  A compression garment may help and I strongly suggest you discuss it with your PS. 
How to Convince Insurance Company to Cover Tummy Tuck?
Insurance pay for tummy tucks
Feb 5, 2020
If your tummy tuck is purely for cosmetic reasons, your insurance company will most likely not pay for it.  If you have a lot of loose lower abdominal skin causing rashes unresponsive to medical care, etc, then they may pay for part or all of it.  I suggest discussing this with your board certified plastic surgeon.

How long do I need to take off work after a tummy tuck?
Recovery after tummy tuck
Feb 5, 2020
Most people take about 2 weeks off of work and their regular activity following a tummy tuck, and no exercising or heavy lifting for about 4-6 weeks.  So since your job is physically demanding you may need to take more time off work. However, recovery periods will vary depending on the amount of surgery and liposuction you have, so I suggest you ask your plastic surgeon.
