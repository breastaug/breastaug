Will a tummy tuck get rid of pregnancy stretch marks?
Apr 14, 2020
Hi and thank you for your question. Based on your photo, you appear to be a great candidate for an abdominoplasty. In a tummy tuck the skin between the umbilicus and public area is removed, therefore any stretch marks in that area are removed. If there are any above the level of the umbilicus they will likely remain after surgery. I recommend you consult with a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon to discuss your concerns further. Best wishes
