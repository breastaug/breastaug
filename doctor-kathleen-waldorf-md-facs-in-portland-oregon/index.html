What are the odds of permanent nerve damage, or even temporary nerve damage post facelift?
nerve damage after facelift
Apr 10, 2018

It is common however to experience some numbness of the face, neck and sometimes ears after a facelift. This usually resolves with time and is rarely permanent as the sensory nerves can regenerate.
