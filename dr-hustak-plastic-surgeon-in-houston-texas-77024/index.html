Weight is stable 8 months after vertical sleeve gastrectomy. Can I get a tummy tuck and BBL?
Best to wait at least 18 months
Jun 22, 2020
After massive weight loss surgery, we usually recommend 18 months after surgery with 6 months of stable weight within ideal +/- 5 lbs. However, surgery can be done before the 18 month marker, but the outcome is less predictable as the body is still acclimating to the changes of massive weight loss.

After pregnancy, is there any specific procedure to return the waist to an hourglass not just flatten
Combination of procedures can help
Jun 22, 2020
If you had an hourglass figure before, then quite often this can be re-established with a combination of muscle tightening, skin removal, liposuction, and often fat grafting to the hips; procedures which help to redefine the hourglass shape. Can I get a hiatal hernia repair with a tummy tuck?
Staging the procedures
Jun 22, 2020
Traditionally these are staged since they are accessed differently. Doing a hiatal hernia repair, usually treated laparoscopically, works by inflating the abdomen. A tummy tuck, however, tightens the abdominal wall and would be difficult to perform if the abdomen is swollen from the air of the laparoscopic procedure. Therefore, we traditionally stage these procedures.
Kristi Hustak, MD
Board Certified Plastic Surgeon
