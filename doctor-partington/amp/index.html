Should I do labia surgery and lipo at same time?
Should I do labia surgery and lipo at same time?
Feb 14, 2019
Combining Labiaplasty and liposuction at the same time is very reasonable. Be aware that post operative swelling in the vaginal area may be more pronounced, especially if liposuction of the abdomen has been performed. When indicated, I often combine labiaplasty and liposuction of the mons pubis to enhance results.

How long will the pain of swelling last? Is it normal to lose stitches after labiaplas
Painful after labiaplasty
Aug 17, 2018
It sounds like you may be experiencing local irritation from outside absorbable sutures. Besides soaking and analgesics, it might be worthwhile to ask your surgeon if he/she might trim excess suture material at the external knots which may be causing chafing and consequent inflammation. It is this inflammation more likely than infection that causes the pain. Often Epsom salt soaks can help relieve swelling and discomfort.
I’m sorry you’re uncomfortable . I hope this helps.
