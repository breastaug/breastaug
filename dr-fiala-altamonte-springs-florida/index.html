I’m getting a breast augmentation but do I need a lift?
Breast lift
Sep 23, 2019
As you probably have suspected, you will get a nicer breast shape and nipple position if you do a lift as well as your planned augmentation.
Is it normal for my implants to still look like this 6 weeks out? What can I do?
Trans-ax riding high
Sep 20, 2019
One of the drawbacks of the trans-axillary technique, if it is performed without an endoscope, is that it can be difficult to get a good release of the pocket inferiorly.  This can lead to a high-riding implant.
Try the bandeau, but if it doesn't start dropping by 3-4 months out, you might have to consider re-adjusting the pocket surgically.
What can be done with this extra skin?
Complex revision
Sep 16, 2019
Hi.  It looks as though your symmastia revision is holding up fine, but you have recurrent laxity of just the medial infra-mammary skin.  Although it's a little hard to tell in the photos, it may benefit from re-tightening the skin, using the medial part of your breast lift incision.  It might require extending your scar, though.  Return to your surgeon to ask.
Blue, purple, red veins appeared around my eye sockets & eyelids. What could be the probl
Periorbital veins
Sep 16, 2019
Hi.  It looks like you have normal veins together with fairly translucent skin.  If they bother you, and you don't want to simply use a cosmetic cover-up, you could have the veins treated with laser.  See a Board-certified plastic surgeon or dermatologist with laser experience in your area.
Do I need an Implant or Implant/Lift?
Breast lift
Sep 13, 2019
As far as I can tell from you photos, I think a short-scar breast lift would really add to the result, lifting the nipple to a more youthful position.  I'd be worried that after an augmentation alone, the nipple would still be low, and the breast somewhat saggy.  If you are not sure, you can always do the lift at a second stage.
Lipo 360 with thighs or will I need a tummy tuck?
Liposuction
Sep 7, 2019
Hi.  Looking at your photos, it seems like you don't have that much tissue laxity.  So, I don't think you need a tummy tuck - liposuction of the flanks and abdomen would, IMO, be the better choice.Of course, an in-person exam is needed to confirm all this.Best wishes
Want to replace implants for smaller size but have keloid scarring? Options?
Wide-spread scars
Sep 7, 2019
Hi.  Your scar pattern is actually not a "keloid", but what is called a "wide-spread scar".  This is where the scar widens out over time, either due to tension on the skin, or from simply having thin, stretchy tissues.If you downsize and re-do some of the lift, the old scar could be removed.  But there is a chance that the newer incision could also end up looking like this, rather than being a nice, narrow and neat incision.
What do I do when Botox is no longer taking care of my nasty 11?
Strong 11's
Aug 30, 2019
Adding some filler to those pesky "11" lines, in addition to Botox, is the next step.If your physician wasn't aware of that, best to seek a second opinion.
I had DVT in my leg twice is it safe for somebody who takes blood thinners regularly?
DVT risk
Aug 29, 2019
Hi. With a history of 2 DVT's, I would suggest you see a hematologist to assess your level of risk for surgery, and to recommend what kind of post-op DVT prevention might be best, given your specific case.

Some surgeries, like a tummy tuck, might be unwise.

Why do I feel pain on top of the left breast upon touch 6 years post-op?
Post-op Sensory nerve issue
Aug 29, 2019
Hi.  Your hypersensitivity to light touch which is localized to a very specific area sounds like you may have a small sensory nerve that is unhappy, and causing some disordered sensations.  You could ask your surgeon about a diagnostic injection of some local anesthesia into the trigger point, and if it relieves the pain temporarily, you have confirmed the diagnosis.  Neurontin tablets may help with this as well.
Tumescent local and IV sedation safe for a tummy tuck?
Anesthesia for tummy tuck
Aug 28, 2019
Personally, we use general anesthesia for our tummy tucks, together with an Exparel TAP block.I prefer full muscle relaxation when I am performing the abdominal plication phase of the surgery.

Inflammatory symptoms
Aug 28, 2019
Personally, I would hold off on implants.  What if your symptoms got worse after your saline augmentation?  Have you considered fat grafting to the breast instead?
I feel like I need an extended tummy tuck but I don't think I need a breast lift though?
Tummy tuck
Aug 27, 2019
Hi.  Your photos look like you are a good candidate for a tummy tuck, with some liposuction of the flanks and back-fat.  It is likely you will need the extended incision, due to the amount of laxity that you have.  Since there are no photos of your back, I can't assess what's happening there.Best, as always, to have an in-person consult with an experienced board-certified plastic surgeon to discuss your issues.All the best,
How can I be sure everything is done correctly like sterilization cleanliness due to surg
Procedures done correctly.
Aug 26, 2019
Hi.  Have your procedure in an accredited facility - a hospital, surgery center, or office surgery suite inspected by AAAASF, AAAHC or JCAHO.  Have a tour first.  Ask what their nosocomial infection rate is.  If you don't like what you see, trust your gut feeling - and keep looking.
I Have 1000cc`s Saline over Filled with 850 Implant Bag, How Can I Proceed To Meet My Exp
Complex post-op situation
Aug 25, 2019
Hi.  I would deflate your saline implants percutaneously in the office, and let the tissue re-drape, for at least 4 weeks.Then, I would plan a breast lift, to get your nipple back to the correct position, and to remove any residual loose skin.  Finally, you could place a more proportional gel implant, behind the muscle.It's a lot of work.  And there's no good way to do it without the lift.
