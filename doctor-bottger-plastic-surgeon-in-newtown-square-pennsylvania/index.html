Can a doctor change the spacing between breasts?
Can a doctor change spacing between breasts?
Nov 8, 2016
This is a frequent concern of patients undergoing breast augmentation. The amount of space between the breasts will depend on every patients individual anatomy. If there is very minimal breast tissue toward the medial part of the chest there is a higher likelihood of a wider space between the augmented breasts. This is the case even with perfectly positioned implants and in certain patients is unavoidable. Hope this is helpful.
