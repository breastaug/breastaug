lift with implant
Jun 23, 2019
As you have said it, I think a peri areolar lift if with an implant should be enough for you. Once the implant fills in the loss volume a small lift like the periareolar lift should be all you need.
How much bigger should I go?
dont need to be bigger
Jun 23, 2019
If you are already D then probably don't need further augmentation, bigger breasts than now can lead to other problems like neck and back pain.
Will I ever get my nipple senses back or is it lost permanently?
unlikely to return now.
Jun 23, 2019
If by three years after surgery the sensation still hasn't returned, it is probably a permanent loss/decreased in sensation.
Birth control and breast augmentation?
dont' think they are related.
Jun 23, 2019
To my knowledge I don't think there is any study so far that links capsule contracture with birth control. Many women who have breast implants also take birth control so I dont' think it will be a problem .
Can I just get augmentation? No lift wanted.
It is possible
Jun 23, 2019
It is possible to get away with just augmentation but to have a lifted look you will likely need large implants, and large implants over time will cause other problems like neck/back pain. so depending how big maybe a lift is a better choice.
Is 65 years old to old to have a mastopexy?
def not too old
Jun 23, 2019
Age is not really a factor if the patient is healthy. Many cosmetic surgeries are performed on older patients (like facelift). It is the general health condition of the patient that is more important.

lift with a small aug
Jun 23, 2019
I think the best solution will be a breast lift and with small implants so you wont' be overly large and also lifted at the same time.
8 weeks post-op and right breast isn't dropping. PS said it might drop. Thoughts?
definitely still possible
Jun 23, 2019
I think it is still possible to change shape and position two months after surgery. I would suggest more aggressive massage and pushing the right side downward. If by three months it still hasn't changed shape, it's likely capsule is already formed by that time and changing position in any major way will be difficult.
My boobs are saggy, how can I make it look natural and big?
lift with augmentation
Jun 23, 2019

How should I resolve my breast asymmetry?
possible to achieve
Jun 23, 2019
The real surgical plan can only be formed after an in person examination but I think it's obvious that left side needs augmentation and right side may need a lift/reduction to make the left depending on the desired size.
Would a breast augmentation or fat transfer with a breast lift improve the look of my breasts?
fat grafting is probably not feasible
Jun 23, 2019
Since your are quite slim, fat grafting is probably not an available option for you. To make a difference you will need at least a few hundred cc of fat injected to each breast, and if you are extremely slim, it is difficult to harvest enough fat for that. A small implant can give a fuller look and also at the same time adjust the fold to crease more symmetry.
What would work best for me for a natural looking breast augmentation?
should be very achievable
Jun 23, 2019
The key for natural looking breasts is to choose implants that are fitting to your chest dimensions. Breast augmentation often looks fake when overboard implants were used, too large for the breast diameter/chest diameter.
