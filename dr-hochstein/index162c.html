How much noticeable volume between 350cc and 385cc? Sientra HP round.
picking an implant size
Apr 15, 2019
The difference between a 350cc implant and 385cc implant is similar to a quarter of a cup size difference.  It is not significant at all. I always tell patients to go with the larger size if they are stuck between the two because if a patient ever comes back to me for a second surgery, it is wishing they went with a bigger size.
Is lipo included in a full tummy tuck and do I need it for my body shape?
tummy tuck with liposuction
Apr 15, 2019
This is important question to ask your surgeon directly. Liposuction can or cannot be included with your surgical quote. I always include liposuction of the flanks with all my tummy tuck surgeries, however, every facility and surgeon may charge differently.

Without photos, it is hard to give a proper assessment.  However, post liposuction patients do experience a lot of swelling. It isn't common right after surgery not to see results due to inflammation. Typically speaking, swelling typically subsides within 3-6 months post surgery.

Can a breast lift, augmentation and BBL be done at once?
BBL & BL with Implants
Apr 15, 2019
A breast lift with an implant and a BBL surgery, most certainly can be done at the same time. However, it is important that you have all required medical tests completed prior to surgery to ensure you are healthy and medically cleared to proceed forward.
Can I get away with just breast augmentation without a breast lift?
breast augmentation vs breast lift
Apr 15, 2019
After viewing your photos, I would highly recommend a lift.  An Augmentation will not lift the breast and with the size of the implant you are interested in, the weight of the implant will weigh down the breast more (resulting in saggier larger breast).
Over 42000.00 for breast reduction, does this seem excessive?
Breast Reduction procedure
Jan 13, 2019
Before a procedure, the pricing should have been clearly outlined to you. $42,000 is extremely high for a breast reduction surgery especially if there were no complications.  I charge typically $5,900-$8,900 for this type of procedure which is done in my AHCA certified facility (private hospital setting).I would recommend to get a  break down of all charges so you have them for your records.
