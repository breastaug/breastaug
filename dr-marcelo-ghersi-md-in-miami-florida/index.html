Will smoking marijuana affect general anesthesia in any way?
Stopping marijuana before surgery
Jul 5, 2018
It is a good idea to stop marijuana use before rhinoplasty. If you had some a few days before surgery, it should not be a big deal. However, discuss it with your surgeon as every case is unique. Good luck.

Nice result
Jul 5, 2018
Hello. From your pictures, I see that you have a nice result. The nose may contract a bit more in the months to come. Talk to your doctor about 5FU and Kenalog injections to expedite that process. If after a year you are not happy with its appearance, you may have to consider revision surgery.

Time will tell

One of the toughest parts of the rhinoplasty recovery process is the wait time after surgery to see your final results. It will take a couple of months before you see your result and are able to critique its new appearance.

Tough question to answer

Hello. The nose changes with time, as does every part of your body. If it is bothering you this much, you might want to consider a consult with a rhinoplasty specialist to find out surgical enhancement options available. 
Rhinoplasty Recovery
Healing times
Jul 5, 2018
Hello. Typically it takes a week for the immediate recovery from rhinoplasty. At that time, you can go back to work. For about a month or so, your nose will be swollen, you will not be able to exercise or do any strenuous activity for this period of time. 
Is my nose damaged or fractured ?
Difficult to tell on your description
Jul 5, 2018
If the nose is not deviated, and your breathing is unchanges, probably it is not a major injury. It is close to impossible to know if there is a fracture unless you get a CT or MRI. Give it some time, unless you have one of the symptoms above, then I would go see a specialist right away.
Rhinoplasty Recovery Time and Downtime?
Varies tremendously
Jul 5, 2018
Hello. This depends on the extent of the operation you had done. Typically we leave sutures/bandages/casts on for a week, at which time you can go out in public. But your nose will be swollen or inflammed for a few weeks to months after your procedure. 
How much will it cost to have a rhinoplasty in New York?
Cost vary
Jul 5, 2018
Hello. Costs vary greatly between surgeons and between cities. Typically NY is one of the most expensive cities in the country for plastic surgery procedures. If you are on a budget, I recommend looking at equally qualified doctors in other, less expensive markets. 
How To Treat Hole In Nose From Cocaine Use?
Saddle nose
Jul 5, 2018
This is a common sequelae of cocaine abuse, unfortunately. If the septal perforation is small and not symptomatic there is nothing to do. If it causes symptoms or is large enough to cause a collapse of your nose and a saddle deformity, it will have to be fixed surgically by a rhinoplasty expert.
When to Stop Drinking Alcohol Prior to Surgery?
One week should be enough
Jul 5, 2018
Assuming you are talking about sporadic social drinking, one week should suffice to get the negative effects of alcohol out of your system prior to rhinoplasty.
