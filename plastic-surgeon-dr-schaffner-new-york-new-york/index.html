Is it possible to get a mommy makeover with no drains on you tuck and breast lift?
Tummy tucks without drains / Breast lifts without drains - DEFINITELY POSSIBLE!
Sep 23, 2019
It is possible to not need draining after a tummy tuck and/or breast lift.  That is a decision to be made by the board certified plastic surgeon at the conclusion of the procedure prior to closure.  Progressive tension sutures during a tummy tuck and pillar sutures during a breast lift help minimize the need for a drain or the length of time the drain is needed.  Please speak with your surgeon about these suture techniques prior to your surgery.
