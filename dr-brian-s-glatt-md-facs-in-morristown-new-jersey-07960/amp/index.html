Are my breast implants bottoming out?
Oct 5, 2018
Good question! Judging by the photographs you appear to present signs of bottoming out.   When an implant loses its internal support or when your skin is not able to hold the implant properly in place , it can begin to sink towards the lower part of the breast area.
