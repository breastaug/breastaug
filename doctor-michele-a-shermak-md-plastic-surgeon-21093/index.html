Should tummy tuck incision be covered post-op? What is better for healing?
Tummy Tuck Incision management
Jul 22, 2020
Hello!
Initially I think it is nice to cover the incisions. For the first 5 days I use an emollient gauze to moisturize. For several weeks after this I use tape. After that I move to lotion and open to air. Especially early, I like to keep the incisions covered to prevent any rubbing on them and allow the scars to mature most optimally.
Each plastic surgeon has her/his preference. I would recommend you discuss this with your surgeon who will be happy to share her/his recommendations.
