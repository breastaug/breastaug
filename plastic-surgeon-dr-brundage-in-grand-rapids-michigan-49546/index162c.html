What would be the right option when it comes to fixing a upright nose?
Short Nose
May 4, 2020
The actual name for your type of nose is a “short nose”. The “short” is from the tip to the nasion (where you nose takes off from your forehead) length. To improve this it entails a derotation which would include release from your septum and possible cartilage struts to hold it “down”. This is not a common operation so be sure you see someone versed in this type rhinoplasty. At the same time you would try to get a bit of a tip set back and refinement of your somewhat bulbous tip.Best of luck.
